# **1. Paid Time Off & Leave Operations Policy**

## **1.1 Outpatient Sick Time & Hospitalization Leave**
- **Paid Outpatient Sick Leave Entitlement**: Full-time employees are entitled to up to **14 days** of paid outpatient sick leave per calendar year, compensated at 100% of base salary.
- **Hospitalization Leave Entitlement**: Employees requiring inpatient hospitalization or doctor-mandated recovery are entitled to up to **60 days** of paid hospitalization leave per calendar year (inclusive of the 14 days outpatient sick leave).
- **Medical Certificate (MC) Submission Deadline**: For sick leave exceeding **2 consecutive workdays**, a Medical Certificate from a registered medical practitioner must be submitted within **48 hours** of taking the leave. Failure to submit within 48 hours may result in the absence being reclassified as unpaid leave.

## **1.2 Paid Vacation & Annual Leave Accrual**
- **Standard Vacation Accrual**: Employees accrue **14 days** of paid annual vacation leave per year for 1-3 years tenure, **18 days** for 4-7 years tenure, and **21 days** for 8+ years tenure.
- **12-Hour Shift Accrual Rate**: Employees working 12-hour shift rotations accrue vacation at a rate where 1 shift day equals **1.5 standard vacation days**.

## **1.3 Bereavement & Compassionate Leave**
- **Immediate Family Bereavement**: Employees are granted **5 consecutive workdays** of paid bereavement leave for the passing of immediate family members (spouse, child, parent, sibling).
- **Extended Family Bereavement**: Employees are granted **3 consecutive workdays** of paid bereavement leave for extended family members (grandparent, parent-in-law).
