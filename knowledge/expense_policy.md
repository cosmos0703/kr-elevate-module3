# **2. Expense & Travel Guidelines Policy**

## **2.1 Home Office Equipment Expenses**
- **Noise-Canceling Headphones**: Full-time employees with active remote or hybrid work agreements may expense up to **$150 USD** for noise-canceling headphones for remote communication. Pre-approval from line manager is required.
- **Ergonomic Accessories**: Keyboards, mice, and monitor stands are reimbursable up to **$100 USD** once every 24 months.

## **2.2 Business Gifts & Hospitality**
- **Client & Host Family Gifts**: Business gifts to external partners, clients, or host families are reimbursable up to **$50 USD** per gift, subject to manager approval.
- **PROHIBITED GIFTS**: Cash, cash equivalents, pre-paid debit cards, and **gift cards** are **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** regardless of amount.
- **PROHIBITED ENTERTAINMENT**: Adult entertainment, **room salons**, gambling, and illegal activities are **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** from expense reimbursement regardless of dollar amount or approval status.
