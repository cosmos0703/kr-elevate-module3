# **3. Remote & Hybrid Work Policy**

## **3.1 Remote Status & Work From Home (WFH)**
- **Hybrid Work Agreement**: Employees working in approved hybrid roles may work remotely up to **2 days per week** with manager sign-off.
- **Home Office Monitor Provisioning**: Employees verified as Full Remote or Hybrid (2+ days WFH) are eligible for one corporate external monitor (up to 27-inch) delivered to their registered home shipping address via ServiceImmediately ticket procurement.
