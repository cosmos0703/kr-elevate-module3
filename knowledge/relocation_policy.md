# **4. Relocation & Global Mobility Policy**

## **4.1 International Relocation Allowance**
- **Relocation Allowance Limit**: Employees approved for international office transfers are eligible for a lump-sum relocation allowance up to **$5,000 USD** for temporary housing and logistics.
- **Address & Badge Procedures**: Employees relocating must update their home address in WorkWeek within **5 business days** of arrival and open a ServiceImmediately ticket for new building badge access.
